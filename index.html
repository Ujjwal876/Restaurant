Welcome to Jevelin Restaurant

We believe that food is a celebration of life and that traditional foods are an enduring part of our culture. Family owned and operated, we challenge ourselves everyday to live up to our name by preparing and serving traditional, Indian home style meals with devotion, elegance, and grace. Each of us has special memories of home-made-food - the kind that keeps us longing for it when we are away from home. Capturing this authentic flavor has become a critical undertaking for Jevelin and we are proud to continue to cook traditional meals from recipes perfected from over thousands of years.

An Indian food-lover’s dream, Jevelin's food is prepared for you using the finest seasonal ingredients in separate vegetarian by our specially trained chefs. Each menu item is specifically tailored with flair and attention to detail is characteristic of Jevelin for every occasion, whether it be a large dinner party, family outing, intimate first date.
